Web Development

We offer high quality, custom built websites and web applications, working with you to bring your ideas to life. With full control from the ground up, we can build you the exact site you want. No loss of functionailty, no corners cut. When building working with you we work towards 3 key values in order to make sure your website is the best tht it can be. These are:

Fast - One of the most important factors for SEO and user experience is a site that loads quickly. Studies have shown that a one-second delay in page load times results in around 10% fewer conversions. With this in mind, we make sure that every site we build loads as fast as possible.

Mobile-First - With over 60% of users now primarily accessing the internet through a mobile device, a mobile-first approach has never been so necessary. All our sites are fully responsive and look great on any device.

SEO - All of our websites are built with Search Engine Optimisation best practices in mind. We understand the importance of being visible online and will do our part to make you and your new website are found easily and often.

Wordpress Websites

Wordpress is one of, if not the most widely used CMS (Content Management System) around today. With 30% of ALL websites on the internet today currently using Wordpress, you can rest assured you are using a tried, tested and trusted technology. For some people, having full control of their website after launch and being able to update it by themselves regularly and often can be a big deal and a big help. Although the speed and customisability may never truly rival that of a completely custom built website, you can still have a site with an amazing design and brilliant functionailty through the help of plugins. Here are some points so you can decide whether a Wordpress powered website is right for you:

Control - As mentioned earlier, one of the main benefits of a Wordpress website is control. Being able to make changes as and when you want. For many changes, little-to-no coding knowledge is needed. For anything larger, we're always here to help.

Plugins - Wordpress has an absolutely massive library of plugins, allowing you to add practically any functionality you could think of to your website. Take your business in a new direction and need to offer another service? No problem! Wordpress has you covered.

Blogging - Blogging can be a great way to offer value to your customer base, all while improving your visability through SEO. With all Wordpress sites coming with blogging functionality built in, starting a blog has never been easier.
